One of thee black movies from my childhood. Growing up in my type of community, most movies were just white/caucasian peopl, so when I watch movies like Best amn, and a lot of Tyler Perry movies, I felt a connection that made me fall inlove with film even more, as I was now seeing black people on my screen, and not just black but dark-skinned people.
